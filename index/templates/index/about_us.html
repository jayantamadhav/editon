About Us

Editon Consortium Publishing is an international scholarly publisher for open access journals. EC Publishing aims at providing exemplary scholarly publishing for academics and researchers. It envisions sustainable production and dissemination of knowledge resources globally.

About the Journals

Editon Consortium Publishing Journals are published online. They cover areas in humanities, arts, and social sciences; specifically, business, management, human resource, literature, communication, mass media studies, curriculum and educational studies, economics, development studies, languages, linguistics, psychology and Kiswahili. We are welcoming Journal proposals from other fields. Should you have a proposal for a Journal in your field, contact the Managing Editor.

Our Mission

Editon Consortium Publishing is committed to research advancement through publishing high-quality research and availing it to the global community. More importantly, is the mandate to ensure every scholar and researcher contributes to the scientific knowledge repository.

Editorial Policy

Editon consortium publishing journals policies are outlined in detail in the author guidelines. Authors are advised to follow the guidelines strictly to avoid manuscript rejection. All manuscripts are to be sent to the editor through editonltd@gmail.com and copied to editoncjournals@gmail.com.

Publication Calendar

Editon consortium journals’ articles are published online once a manuscript is through the review process. Publication on rolling basis eliminates the backlog and quickens the publication process.
